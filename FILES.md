# Conventions

The following enumerations are used in file names:

## Characters
* 00 - Picard
* 01 - Riker (number one, of course)
* 02 - Data
* 03 - Troi
* 04 - Worf
* 05 - Crusher
* 06 - La Forge
* 07 - Carlstrom
* 08 - Butler
* 09 - Computer
* ff - other characters

## Worlds
* 00 -
* 02 - Allanor
* 03 - Morassia
* 04 - Mertens Orbital Station
* 05 - Frigis (Shonoisho Epsilon VI)
* 06 - Unity Device
* 07 - Horst III
* 10 - Combat
* 5f - Enterprise Bridge
